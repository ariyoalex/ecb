Our Story

Crafting Excellence
Since 1976

Founded in 1976 by John Bumgarner in Springfield, Missouri, Executive Coach Builders has grown from a two-model limousine specialist to America's premier luxury transportation manufacturer. Under the ownership of David Bakare since 1993, ECB has expanded to more than 25 different vehicles and custom modifications — serving clients across the United States and worldwide.

With a dedicated team whose plant managers and supervisors alone possess 200 years of combined experience, ECB has built an unwavering reputation for quality, customer service, and innovation. Every vehicle is QVM certified, made in the USA, and engineered to exceed your expectations.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

1976
Founded in Springfield, MO

John Bumgarner establishes Executive Coach Builders, initially specializing in just two limousine models — laying the foundation for a dynasty of luxury vehicle manufacturing.

Roger Farris Expands Operations

New ownership drives rapid growth. ECB becomes known for distinct quality as Farris — himself a limousine consumer — brings a customer-first philosophy to manufacturing.

1981
1986
400+ Limousines Annually

ECB hits a major milestone, producing over 400 limousines a year. LCT Magazine features the company, recognizing it as one of the nation's premier builders.

David Bakare Takes the Helm

Under new ownership, ECB embarks on its most ambitious era — expanding the product line, opening new markets, and establishing ECB as the definitive luxury transportation manufacturer.

1993
Today
600+ Vehicles & 25+ Models

ECB produces over 600 vehicles per year across 25+ models, serving clients worldwide. The legacy of quality, craftsmanship, and innovation continues to define every vehicle that leaves the Springfield facility.
